Biography

Dr. Xiao Xiao received the B.S. and M.S. degrees from the Shandong University of Technology, Shandong, China, in 2010 and 2012, respectively, and the Ph. D degree from University of Macau, Macau, China, in 2017, all in Electromechanical Engineering. He was a Research Fellow with the Department of Biomedical Engineering, National University of Singapore, Singapore. He is currently a Research Assistant Professor with the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Southern University of Science and Technology, China.

In recent years, Dr. Xiao Xiao has participated in one Singapore Technology Acceleration Program, one National key R & D project, one NFSC project, and two Macao FDCT projects. He has published more than 30 international journal and conference papers and contributed 4 book chapters. He has applied 9 Chinese patents, 2 PCT patents, and 1 Singapore patent, among which 4 patents had been authorized.
